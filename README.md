# Smart Study Planner AI
Final project for the Building AI course

## Summary
Smart Study Planner AI helps students organize study schedules and deadlines efficiently. This Building AI course project uses AI to give personalized study suggestions and reminders.

## Background
Many students struggle to manage study schedules and forget assignments or exams.  
This is common in high schools and universities.  
Personal motivation: I have experienced stress from juggling multiple deadlines and want to make learning easier.  

* Students waste time deciding what to study first
* Deadlines are often missed
* Study plans are not personalized

## How is it used?
Students input courses, assignments, and exam dates.  
AI generates study schedules, reminders, and priorities.  
Users: High school/college students, parents, teachers.
